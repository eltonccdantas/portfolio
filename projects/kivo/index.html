    The story behind

    KIVO

    KIVO was born from a real need: sharing a video on WhatsApp without depending on an online service that would store your file on some unknown server.

    The core idea is simple — your file privacy belongs to you. Document photos, personal videos, PDFs with sensitive information: none of that needs to pass through third parties just to get smaller.

    Built with Flutter, KIVO runs natively on Android, iOS, macOS, Windows, and Linux from a single codebase. Compression is done with well-established open‑source libraries — FFmpeg, flutter_image_compress, and iText — all operating entirely on your device.

    The original file is never modified. KIVO reads the source file, creates a compressed copy in the location you choose, and nothing else.
