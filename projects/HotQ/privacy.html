    HotQ collects no data

    See below all the details about how HotQ handles (or rather, doesn't handle) your information.

    Last updated: March 30, 2026

    🔒 Summary: HotQ does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device, with no connection to external servers.

    1. Data Collection

    HotQ collects no personal data whatsoever. There is no usage analytics, telemetry, error reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind in the application.

    We have no knowledge of who you are, which screenshots you capture, or how you use the app.

    2. File Processing

    All screenshot capture, recording, and file operations are performed entirely on your device, with no external processing. Your screenshots and recordings are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

    HotQ does not require an internet connection to function. No network requests are made during normal use of the application.

    The original files on your system are never modified without your explicit action. HotQ only creates new files (screenshots/recordings) in the locations you specify.

    3. Permissions

    HotQ requests only the permissions strictly necessary to perform its functions:

    macOS

    • Screen Recording: allows capturing screenshots and screen recordings.
    • Accessibility: enables global hotkeys and overlay functionality.
    • Files and Folders: allows saving screenshots/recordings to chosen locations.

    Windows

    • Screen Capture: allows capturing screenshots and screen recordings.
    • Global Hotkeys: enables keyboard shortcuts system-wide.
    • File System Access: allows saving files to chosen locations.

    Linux

    • Screen Capture: allows capturing screenshots and screen recordings.
    • Global Hotkeys: enables keyboard shortcuts system-wide.
    • File System Access: allows saving files to chosen locations.

    No permission is used for any purpose other than those described above.

    4. Children's Privacy

    HotQ does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable digital consent age in your jurisdiction). The application does not contain features directed at children that require special data handling under laws such as COPPA, GDPR-K, or similar regulations.

    5. Third-Party Services

    HotQ does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or cloud storage providers.

    The application uses only open-source libraries (screen_capturer, hotkey_manager, window_manager, etc.) that operate entirely on the device and make no external connections.

    6. Data Security

    Because HotQ never transmits or stores your data outside your device, the risk of a data breach originating from HotQ is zero. Your screenshots and recordings remain under your full control at all times.

    7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

    Because HotQ collects no personal data, there is no stored data to access, correct, export, or delete. All processed files remain on your device and can be managed using your operating system's standard tools.

    Since no personal data is collected or processed, HotQ has no obligations under GDPR, CCPA, or similar data protection regulations.

    8. Open Source

    HotQ is free and open source software. The source code is available on GitHub for anyone to review, audit, or contribute to. This transparency ensures that our privacy claims can be independently verified.
